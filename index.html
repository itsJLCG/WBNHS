Junior High School (Grades 7-10)

Our Junior High School program provides a strong foundation for students, ensuring they are academically prepared for Senior High School. Students will receive a balanced curriculum that focuses on core subjects such as English, Math, Science, and Social Studies, along with enrichment activities that help them grow socially and emotionally.

Grade Levels: 7, 8, 9, 10

Senior High School (Grades 11-12)

Our Senior High School program offers specialized tracks that prepare students for their chosen career path. The program is designed to give them the skills, knowledge, and experience they need for success in higher education or the workforce. We offer the following tracks:

  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) - For students interested in pursuing careers in science and technology fields.
  • HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) - For students who wish to pursue careers in law, politics, education, or communications.
  • CSS (Computer Science and Software) - For students aiming to work in technology and software development.
  • CP (Computer Programming) - For students interested in developing software applications, coding, and working in the IT industry.
  • TechVoc (Technical Vocational Education and Training) - For students preparing to enter the workforce directly after graduation with hands-on training in trades like automotive, culinary, or carpentry.
